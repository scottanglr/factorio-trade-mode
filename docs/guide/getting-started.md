# Getting Started

This is the fastest way to get from a fresh game to your first working trade loop.

## 1. Craft A Trade Box

The `Trade Box` recipe is available from the start and uses:

- `1x Steel Chest`
- `2x Electronic Circuit`

## 2. Place And Open The Box

- Place the `Trade Box` where you want deliveries to happen.
- Open it to bring up the `Trade order` side panel.
- That panel is the control surface for the box's current buy order.

## 3. Create Your First Buy Order

1. Pick the item you want.
2. Enter a positive whole-number price.
3. Optionally click `Use suggested` to fill a baseline price.
4. Click `Save order`.

Once saved, the box becomes a live buyer for that item.

## 4. Open The Global Market Window

You can open the main window through either of these entry points:

- The `Trade Market` shortcut in the shortcut bar.
- The `Toggle Trade Market` control, if you prefer using a hotkey.

The main window contains:

- `Market` for browsing buy orders.
- `Contracts` for player jobs.
- `Economy` for money flow and UBI.
- `Admin` for diagnostics if you are an admin.

## 5. Fund Your Spending

Orders only settle if the buyer can afford them. In practice, money enters the system from two places:

- `UBI`, which is paid out to connected players.
- Other players paying you through deliveries or contracts.

## 6. Try A First Delivery

- Ask another player to drop the requested item into your box.
- Or set up an inserter so it feeds the box automatically.
- If the order is active and you can afford the delivery, payment happens automatically.

## 7. Try A First Contract

1. Open the `Contracts` tab.
2. Fill in a title, a reward, and a short briefing.
3. Create the contract.
4. Let another player assign themselves.
5. Pay them when the work is done.

## Good First Checks

- Open the `Market` tab and confirm your listing appears.
- Open the `Economy` tab and confirm the game is generating UBI.
- Select a delivery inserter to see whether it is accumulating payout stats.
